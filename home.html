About Us

Barangay SK is a vibrant and dynamic organization dedicated to representing the interests and aspirations of the youth within our barangay. SK stands for Sangguniang Kabataan, which translates to Youth Council in English. We are the grassroots level of government tasked with addressing the concerns and needs of young people, fostering their development, and ensuring their active participation in local governance. At Barangay SK, we believe in the power of youth to drive positive change and make meaningful contributions to society. Our organization serves as a platform for young individuals to voice their opinions, advocate for their rights, and take action on issues that matter most to them. Through various initiatives, programs, and projects, we aim to empower youth to become responsible, engaged, and proactive members of the community.
